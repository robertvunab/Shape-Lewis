Guidelines

This will help you see the Lewis structure of the molecule that you type in. Here are the guidelines for typing numbers in:

  1. Any number would go as a subscript. For example, dihydrogen oxide would be H2O.
  2. In order to write superscripts, write use ^(superscript). For example, fluorine ion would be F^(-)
  3. Sometimes, the element you are looking for may not be saved in our library. If that happens, please submit that element here: WORKING ON IT!
  4. Press the key "enter" to submit!

Sometimes, a "+" or "-" sign appears on the top right corner of an element: this is the formal charge of the element. Links to formal charge: Wikipedia, and Master Organic Chemistry.
Be aware of isomers: Unless relevant, only one time of isomers are drawn.
Enjoy!
